We are a group of passionate researchers from the City Colleges of Tagaytay who have come together to create Wildboar-BQ, an exceptional culinary experience that celebrates the art of grilling. Inspired by our love for delicious food and the rich grilling traditions of our native Philippines, we embarked on a journey to bring you the finest grilled dishes that will leave you craving for more.

MISSION AND VISION

MISSION:

To be known as the best barbecue in the community and let everyone experience the best-tasting barbecue making it one of the top choices and go-to street food anytime.

VISION:

Wildboar-B-Q aims to provide happiness and joy through good tasting, quality, and affordable food. The researchers are committed to provide the customers with service by demonstrating warmth, respect, and great hospitality to have them come back again. The main goal is to produce high quality food at a great value, affordable delicious barbecue.
