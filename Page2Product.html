GRILLED PORK

GRILLED SQUID

GRILLED CHICKEN

Procedures on how we serve your delicious dishes.

1. Marinate chicken/pork/squid pieces in a mixture of soy sauce, calamansi juice (or lemon juice), garlic, onion, black pepper, and salt for at least 8-10 hours.

2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.

3. Put them into sticks.

4. Grill chicken until skin is charred and internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C), basting with marinade.

5. Let chicken rest for a few minutes.

6. Serve on a platter, garnish with calamansi slices.
